# Project Charter

## Mission
Build an extensible research platform that gathers and validates information about Ohio school districts.

## Version 1 Goals
- Import Ohio school list
- Store data in SQLite
- Research original building year
- Save supporting sources
- Resume interrupted research

## Future Agents
- Building Age
- Renovation History
- Bond History
- OFCC Participation
- Architect History
- Board Minutes
- Opportunity Score

## Success Criteria
Every recorded fact is backed by one or more sources and a confidence score.
